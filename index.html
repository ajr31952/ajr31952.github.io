My Family and My Childhood

My mother's family hails from the great country of Cuba. My mother came from Cuba at the ripe age of seven and lived in Miami. Her name is Mayra Perez Robertson. My father went to North Georgia College and served in Vietnam War as a sergeant. His name is William Carlton Robertson Jr. I have one older brother whose name is William Carlton Robertson III. He graduated from St.Pius X Catholic High School in 2013. He attended Augusta University as a pitcher on a scholarship. My Abuela is the only living grandparent of mine. My cousins and I go to her house every Sunday to eat lunch. Going to lunch every Sunday with my cousins has taught me that family is important and you need to spend time with them. Because of my heritage, my three favorite colors are red, white, and blue because those three colors are represented on both the American flag and the Cuban flag.

Body Measurements and Fitness
